## Activity 1 (Introduction)

Explore the object oriented principles of inheritance and polymorphism, and see how they can add flexibility to object oriented designs.

## Activity 2 (Shape Drawing)

Explore the use of inheritance and polymorphism in the design and development of the shape drawing program.

## Activity 3 (Wrap Up)

Reflect on what you have learnt this week, and examine the tasks to complete in OnTrack.
