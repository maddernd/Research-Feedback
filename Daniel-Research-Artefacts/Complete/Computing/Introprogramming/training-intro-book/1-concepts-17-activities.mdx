## Activity 1 (Coding custom objects)

Discover how to build your own custom objects. Learn about declaring fields and methods and using these to get things done. 

## Activity 2 (Building a water tank)

Explore the syntax needed to declare a class that has a number of fields and methods. Do this by creating an object to play the role of a water storage tank. 

## Activity 3 (Underlying principles)

Discover two of the key principles of object oriented programming: encapsulation and abstraction.

## Activity 4 (Wrap Up)

Reflect on what you have learnt this week, and examine the tasks to complete in OnTrack.
