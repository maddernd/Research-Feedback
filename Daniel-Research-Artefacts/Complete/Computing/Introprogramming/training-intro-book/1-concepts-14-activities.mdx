## Activity 1 (Introduction)

Explore the responsibility driven approach to object oriented development, and its use in designing effective solutions.

## Activity 2 (Documenting a Design)

Examine the process of documenting an object oriented design so that you can share your ideas with others.

## Activity 3 (Making a Drawing Program)

Explore the design and development of a small object oriented drawing program. See how the design ideas come together to make things work.

## Activity 4 (Wrap Up)

Reflect on what you have learnt this week, and examine the tasks to complete in OnTrack.
